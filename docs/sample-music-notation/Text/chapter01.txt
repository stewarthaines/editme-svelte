# Sample Music Notation

The score below is engraved from ABC notation -- a plain-text format for music -- by the `abc2svg` extension installed in this project.

The notation is this chapter's source. Open this book in `SEED.html` and the ABC you see in the editor is everything the score is made from; the frontmatter block sets how the engraving scales at different reading widths, and the lyric line is Georgian (the opening of "Suliko").

```abc2svg
---
scales:
  narrow: 1.9
  wide: 1.15
  full: 0.69
---
M:4/4
%%measurenb 0
L:1/4
Q:1/4=60
V:T clef=treble name=I
V:M clef=treble name=II
V:B clef=bass octave=-2 name=III
%%score (T M) B
K:Gmaj
V:T
cdef|gabc'|]
V:M
CDEF|GABc|]
V:B
CDEF|GABc|]
w:sa-q’va-rlis sa-plavs ve-dze-bdi ver vna-khe! da-k’a-rgul-iq’-o!
```
