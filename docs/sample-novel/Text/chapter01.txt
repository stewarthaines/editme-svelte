[It was the _best_ of times]{.opening} it was the [_wurst_]{.lang-de} of times. That was a language-switching _pain_{.lang-fr} -- or 'play' -- on the German word for _sausage_. _Best sausage time._ There were periods of intense concentration punctuated by deployments. I'm not a prose writer. Mostly software, me.

That doesn't mean anything.

After a few hours of this you might tire and look for an alternative. There won't be one. This is all that's left. At least it isn't A.I.-generated.

"Some text", he said.

"What about now?" she asked.
