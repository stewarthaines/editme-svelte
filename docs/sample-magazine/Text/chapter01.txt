## Make one of your own

This could be *your magazine*. It's built with html/css/javascript, so your designers and developers are the right people to talk to about style and layout.

Then each issue's content is simple images and plain text.

None of that is a claim you have to take on trust: this issue carries its own source. Open it in `SEED.html` and read this page as it was written.
